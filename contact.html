Frequently Asked Questions

Does DataDrain work on Apple Silicon?

Yes. DataDrain is a universal binary that runs natively on both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs.

What macOS version is required?

DataDrain requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later.

Is DataDrain free?

Yes, DataDrain is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Why does DataDrain need to run outside the sandbox?

DataDrain uses system-level tools to read per-app network statistics. These tools require access that isn't available inside the macOS App Sandbox, which is why DataDrain is distributed directly rather than through the Mac App Store.

Does DataDrain slow down my Mac?

No. DataDrain uses less than 1% CPU and minimal memory. It polls network stats every 2 seconds using efficient system APIs.

Can I see historical data?

Yes. DataDrain tracks daily, weekly, and monthly download/upload totals. You can view summaries and clear history at any time.
